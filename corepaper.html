Gentrification and Its Impacts on Communities and Libraries

This paper was written for IS 212 Values and Communities.

The reason I wrote on this topic is because I am experiencing gentrification first-hand by living in Los Angeles. All around me luxury apartments are being built instead of much needed affordable housing for the massive population of people experiencing homelessness in this city. Rent is constantly going up and it is nearly impossible to own a house in my area. By researching this subject, I learned that gentrification is not something new, but something that has been happening for decades. Although it has the ability to "revitalize neighborhoods", those benefits are not necessarily experienced by long-time residents and marginalized populations.
