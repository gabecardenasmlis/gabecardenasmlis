About

Hi, and welcome to my digital portfolio! On this site you will find various papers and projects I have done for my UCLA MLIS program.

I first began working in libraries when I was 18 years old at the UCLA Young Research Library. Since then, I have worked in multiple departments within the UCLA Library. Currently, I work for the UCLA Library Resource Acquistions & Metadata Services Department as the Digital Resources Manager.

The knowledge I have gained and friends I have made in my UCLA MLIS program have been invaluable. I plan to use the skills I have learned to become a leader in the field of information studies and to work with people to help them find the information they need.

In my free time I enjoy playing beach volleyball, cooking for my wife, singing karaoke, and playing with my two labradors, Maui and Kylo.
