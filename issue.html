Improving Information Literacy Programming in Community Colleges:
A Framework For Sucess

An Issue Paper by Gabriel Cardenas

The need for bibliographic Instruction in community colleges grows as technology
advances and methods of information retrieval change. This especially pertains to
a student population that is often underserved and marginalized. By analyzing previous research, this paper will provide a framework for a successful community college information literacy program.
