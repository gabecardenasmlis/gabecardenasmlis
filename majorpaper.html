Librarians and Social Work:
A Deeper Look at an Evolving Profession


This paper was done for IS 213 Current Issues In Librarianship.

"Librarians are finding new and unique ways to serve society and are performing the services that are normally associated with social work so much that libraries are now starting to hire in-house social workers. By no means is this paper stating that librarians can do the job of a social worker. However, as public services fail underserved and marginalized people, librarians have stepped up to help those in need and have expanded their role into the realm of social work."
